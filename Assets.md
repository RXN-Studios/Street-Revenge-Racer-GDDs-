# Technical Assets

## Art Style
* **Style:** Retro-pixel, NES-inspired aesthetics with realistic-ish car designs.
* **Key Assets:** Player cars (4 frames of animation), road tiles, and particle effects for crashes and nitro.

## Audio
* **Music:** Single chiptune tracks created in FamiStudio for each region and context.
* **SFX:** Pure chiptune sounds for engine loops, skids, crashes, and sirens.

## Development Milestones
1. Core driving physics.
2. Basic race logic and traffic.
3. Heat and police AI systems.
4. Garage and upgrade systems.
5. Full story implementation and polish.

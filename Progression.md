# Progression & Upgrades

## Reputation & Money
* **Reputation:** A single number that increases with wins and decreases with busts; used to unlock new story beats and races.
* **Money:** Earned through racing; spent on upgrades, new cars, and fines.

## The Garage
Players can own between 2 to 5 original car designs. You begin with a basic "beater" and eventually unlock your father's legendary car as an endgame heirloom.

## Upgrade Tiers
Upgrades are divided into discrete tiers (Lv1 to Lv3):
* **Engine:** Improves Acceleration and Top Speed.
* **Tires:** Improves Handling.
* **Nitro:** Increases the duration of the speed burst (activated with SPACE).

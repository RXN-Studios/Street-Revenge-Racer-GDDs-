# Story and World

## Plot Overview
[cite_start]The protagonist, Jax Ryder, seeks to expose his father's killer through the underground racing scene[cite: 68]. [cite_start]The story is told via text boxes and follows a 3-Act structure[cite: 69, 70].

## Campaign Structure
* [cite_start]**Act 1:** Introduction to the basics and the first 2-3 rival bosses in Region 1[cite: 71].
* [cite_start]**Act 2:** Increased police heat and conspiracy clues across Regions 2 and 3 (4-5 bosses)[cite: 72].
* [cite_start]**Act 3:** The antagonist reveal and the final race in Region 4 using the legendary heirloom car[cite: 73].

## World Regions
[cite_start]The game features 4 distinct regions[cite: 81]:
1. [cite_start]**Downtown:** Tight city streets[cite: 82].
2. [cite_start]**Industrial:** Wide factory zones[cite: 83].
3. [cite_start]**Highway:** Straight paths for high speed[cite: 84].
4. [cite_start]**Hills:** Twisty, technical roads[cite: 85].

# Story and World

## Plot Overview
The protagonist, Jax Ryder, seeks to expose his father's killer through the underground racing scene. The story is told via text boxes and follows a 3-Act structure.

## Campaign Structure
* **Act 1:** Introduction to the basics and the first 2-3 rival bosses in Region 1.
* **Act 2:** Increased police heat and conspiracy clues across Regions 2 and 3 (4-5 bosses).
* **Act 3:** The antagonist reveal and the final race in Region 4 using the legendary heirloom car.

## World Regions
The game features 4 distinct regions:
1. **Downtown:** Tight city streets.
2. **Industrial:** Wide factory zones.
3. **Highway:** Straight paths for high speed.
4. **Hills:** Twisty, technical roads.
